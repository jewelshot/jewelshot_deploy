# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 27, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Jewelshot ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered jewelry photography platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, name, password (encrypted)
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)
- **Content:** Images you upload, prompts you create, generated images
- **Communications:** Support tickets, feedback, emails

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Analytics:** Aggregated usage statistics (via Plausible Analytics - privacy-focused, no cookies)
- **Cookies:** Essential cookies only for authentication and functionality

### 2.3 Technical Data
- **API Logs:** Request timestamps, operation types, success/failure rates
- **Error Logs:** Technical errors for debugging (no personal data)
- **Performance Metrics:** System performance and optimization data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Process your AI image generation requests
- Manage your account and credits
- Provide customer support
- Send transactional emails (order confirmations, password resets)

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Detect and prevent abuse
- Optimize AI model performance
- Fix bugs and technical issues

### 3.3 Communication
- Send important service updates
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send promotional emails (with your consent, opt-out available)

### 3.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Services
We share data with trusted service providers:

- **Supabase:** Database and authentication (GDPR compliant)
- **FAL.AI:** AI image processing
- **Stripe:** Payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
- **Resend:** Transactional emails
- **Upstash:** Redis hosting for queue system
- **Vercel:** Application hosting
- **Railway:** Background worker hosting
- **Plausible Analytics:** Privacy-focused analytics (no cookies, GDPR compliant)

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal process
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or security threats

### 4.3 Business Transfers
If we merge with or are acquired by another company, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

### 4.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Account Data
- Active accounts: Retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days
- Backup copies: Removed from backups within 90 days

### 5.2 Generated Images
- User-generated images: Retained for 90 days unless deleted by you
- Batch projects: Retained for 180 days or until manually deleted
- You can delete images anytime from your dashboard

### 5.3 Transaction Records
- Credit transactions: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- Payment records: Retained for 7 years (tax compliance)

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Technical Measures
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Authentication:** Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access Control:** Row-level security on database
- **Regular Backups:** Automated daily backups with 30-day retention
- **Monitoring:** Real-time security monitoring and alerts

### 6.2 Organizational Measures
- Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
- Employee training on data protection
- Regular security audits
- Incident response plan

### 6.3 No Absolute Security
While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. You acknowledge and accept this risk.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Object:** Object to processing of your data
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal data we collect
- Know if we sell or share your data (we don't)
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of data sales (not applicable - we don't sell)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### 7.3 Exercising Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **privacy@jewelshot.ai**

We will respond within:
- **GDPR:** 30 days
- **CCPA:** 45 days
